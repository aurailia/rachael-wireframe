The Front Door

The Initial Consultation

A free 15-minute, no-pressure fit conversation, not a therapy session. You share where you are and what you are hoping for, and together you find the right next step, whether that is therapy or something on the Workshops and Retreats side. This is the primary call to action across the whole site. It books through Wix with a terms checkbox and a 24-hour advance-booking rule. No fee and no obligation, just a chance to feel whether it is a fit.

Therapy compliance rule. Therapy is never marketed as packages, a program, a container, a set number of sessions, or a required commitment.
